Medicycle — Connecting Generosity with Need

Millions of people discard unused medicines every day, while others struggle to afford them. Medicycle bridges this gap by creating a community-driven platform where individuals can donate, share, or sell unused medicine — and those in need can request it for free or at minimal cost. Join us in building a healthier, more caring world one medicine at a time.

PLACE YOUR ORDER VIA

List Medicine

If you have unused or surplus medicines at home, instead of letting them expire or go to waste, you can list them on Medicycle. This feature allows users to donate or sell medicines they no longer need, making them available to others who might desperately require them. By listing your medicine, you’re not only reducing medical waste but also becoming a lifeline for someone who may not be able to afford treatment. It’s a simple yet powerful act of kindness that promotes a healthier and more sustainable community.

Find Medicine

Struggling to afford essential medicines? With Medicycle’s Find Medicine feature, you can browse and request available medicines donated or offered at a low cost by others. This option is designed especially for individuals who are financially constrained or facing emergencies. By connecting those in need with those who can help, this platform ensures that no one has to suffer due to lack of access to basic healthcare. It brings hope, support, and a sense of solidarity to people when they need it the most.

FAQs

❓ What is this platform about?
✅ This platform connects people who want to donate unused or surplus medicines with individuals or NGOs in need. It helps reduce medicine waste and supports community health.
❓ Who can use this platform?
✅ Anyone with access to the internet can use this platform—whether you want to donate, request medicine, or are an NGO serving the community.
❓ Is this platform free to use?
✅ Yes, the platform is completely free for both donors and receivers.
❓ How do I list a medicine for donation?
✅ Click the "List Medicine" button, fill in the medicine details (name, expiry date, quantity), and submit the form. Your listing will be visible to users searching for that medicine.
❓ How do I find a specific medicine?
✅ Use the "Find Medicine" button and enter the name of the medicine in the search bar. The system will show a list of matching available donations.
❓ Is there a limit on how many medicines I can request?
✅ There is no strict limit, but we encourage responsible usage to ensure fair distribution among users.

🩺 About MediCycle
